TLDR: Create a product to encourage people to take steps to improve their mental well-being, improve mindset and reduce stress.

Approach

The design process followed the double diamond, the biggest focus for this project was providing value to users as well as making sure that the final output was feasable for manufacture.

I created a range of concepts using a refined success criteria. Through questionnaires, interviews and testing I refined the idea to a wearable electronic device that can be used to assist breathing and relieve panic attacks.

Goals

Create a product to encourage people to take steps to improve their mental well-being, improve mindset and reduce stress.

Outcome

Able to reduce heart rate by up to 30BPM in stressful situations.

Users expressed that they were at least 2 points more relaxed on a scale of 1 - 10 after using the Ripple.
