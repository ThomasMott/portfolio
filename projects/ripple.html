Major project - 2019

Ripple

TLDR:

Ripple is a wearable technology that aims to encourage users to take steps to support their mental well-being, improve positive mindset and reduce stress.

  • Product Design
  • Prototyping
  • Graphics
  • Adobe Suite

Approach

Ripple was developed following the Double Diamond process. The primary focus for this project was to improve the mental well-being of users by promoting and encouraging self-help techniques. The design also needed to be feasible for affordable manufacturing.

I created a range of concepts from peer discussions and brainstorming. Through questionnaires, interviews and concept testing I refined the idea to a wearable electronic device that can be used to assist breathing and relieve panic attacks.

Goals

Create a product to encourage people to take steps to improve their mental well-being, improve mindset and reduce stress.

Outcome

Ripple was designed to be wearable but also fit inside a small comfort item (such as a teddy) for those with sensory processing needs. The device leads the user through guided breathing exercises to reduce anxiety and promote grounding.

Ripple was able to reduce heart rate by up to 30BPM in stressful situations.

Users expressed that they were at least 2 points more relaxed on a scale of 1 - 10 after using the Ripple.
